Frequently Asked Questions

For regular bookings, plumbers typically arrive within 1–3 hours. For emergency bookings, we dispatch within 15 minutes and aim for 30–60 minutes arrival time depending on your location and traffic conditions.

Every plumber goes through a rigorous 5-step verification: ID proof, address verification, professional license check, police background check, and a practical skills assessment. We also conduct regular quality audits and monitor customer ratings.

Our 30-day service warranty covers all workmanship issues. If the same problem recurs within 30 days of the service being completed, we'll send a plumber to fix it at absolutely no extra cost to you. Note: warranty doesn't cover damage from misuse or new, unrelated issues.

Yes! You can cancel or reschedule for free up to 2 hours before your scheduled appointment from your customer dashboard. Cancellations within 2 hours may incur a small fee of ₹50. Emergency bookings cannot be cancelled once a plumber is dispatched.

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, digital wallets, and cash payment after service completion. All digital payments are SSL encrypted and 100% secure.

You can register as a plumber through our Login page by selecting "Join as Plumber". After submitting your documents and passing our verification, you'll be approved within 2–3 business days and start receiving job requests immediately.

The price estimate shown during booking is very accurate for standard issues. The final price may vary slightly if additional materials or extra labor are required beyond the initial scope. Your plumber will always confirm any additional costs before proceeding.
